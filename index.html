Precision Care for a Balanced Mind

Welcome

Welcome to the Precision Care for a Balanced Mind portal, your trusted resource for understanding and navigating your mental health journey. Personalised insights and assessments are provided to empower your self-awareness and well-being. Assessments can be conducted through paper-based or virtual meetings. Support is available every step as you embrace your strengths and challenges.

Our Mission

The mission is to empower individuals facing mental health challenges by helping them better understand themselves. We believe that self-awareness is the first step towards healing and growth. By offering detailed and personalised assessments, we aim to provide the tools and insights necessary for individuals to navigate their mental health journey with confidence and clarity.

World Mental Health Statistics (2024)

1 in 4

People worldwide will experience mental or neurological disorders at some point in their lives.

450 million

People currently suffer from such conditions, making mental disorders among the leading causes of ill health and disability worldwide.

75%

Philippine Mental Health Statistics (2024)

3.3 million

Filipinos suffer from depressive disorders, representing about 3.3% of the population.

1 in 5

Filipinos suffer from mental health problems, indicating a significant need for mental health services in the country.

2nd leading

Cause of death among Filipinos aged 15-29 is suicide, highlighting the urgent need for mental health interventions.

1 mental health specialist

Per 250,000 Filipinos, showing the severe lack of mental health professionals in the country.

8.3%

Of the population suffers from anxiety disorders, making it one of the most common mental health issues in the Philippines.
